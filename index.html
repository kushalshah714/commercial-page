Abbotsford, BC

Building restrictions ruled out any permanent enclosure, but the winery needed year-round patio revenue without sacrificing the open-air character guests come for in summer. SmartScreen motorized shades with clear vinyl panels provided complete wind and rain protection in winter, and retracted entirely when the weather allowed. A compliance constraint became a competitive advantage.

Motorized Screens — SmartScreen
Year-round patio operation. Full seasonal flexibility. No permanent structure required.
Pacific Institute of Culinary Arts
Vancouver, BC — False Creek

A waterfront dining terrace on False Creek needed weather coverage without obstructing the views that define the venue experience. The school also required a solution that suited the aesthetic of a modern culinary facility. Stobag Resobox retractable awning provided on-demand shade and rain protection with a clean, architectural profile that complements the building.

Retractable Awning — Stobag Resobox
All-weather outdoor dining. Views preserved. Venue aesthetic elevated.

The club needed maximum shade coverage without touching the building or compromising sightlines. Caravita Amalfi Duo cantilever umbrellas delivered full table coverage from a single base, preserving the open character of the space entirely.
